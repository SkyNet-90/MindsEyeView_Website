## ðŸ“… Managing Events (Shows)

### Adding a New Show

1. **Login** to admin panel
2. Click **"Events"** or **"Add New Event"**
3. Fill in the details:
   - **Title**: Name of the event (e.g., "Live at Joe's Bar")
   - **Venue**: Where you're playing
   - **Address**: Full address (helps fans find you)
   - **Date & Time**: When the show starts
   - **Description**: Any special info (cover charge, special guests, etc.)
   - **Ticket URL**: Link to buy tickets (if applicable)
   - **Acoustic Set**: Check this if it's the acoustic duo
4. Click **"Save"**
5. The event appears immediately on your Shows page and homepage!

### Editing or Deleting a Show

1. Go to **Events** in admin panel
2. Find the event you want to change
3. Click **"Edit"** to make changes, or **"Delete"** to remove it
4. Changes are instant!

### Tips for Events

- âœ… Add shows as soon as you book them
