Minds Eye View info

1: Description
Mind’s Eye View is a six-piece high energy classic rock band playing music from the 60’s through the 2000’s. They have been performing in the tri-state area since 2005. The band is now celebrating their 20th anniversary together. With a constantly updated set list, they are perfect for bars, weddings, corporate events, and private parties. Bring your friends, grab a drink and prepare to rediscover the joy of live music. 

Contact page
Mind’s Eye View is currently accepting bookings and would be thrilled to be the life of your next party. For more information on how to book a gig, visit the band's website at https://Mindseyeview.net or get in touch with us on Facebook.

Mind’s Eye View Acoustic
Mind’s Eye View acoustic band consists of 2 members from our full band, Sean Bradley and Mike Markiewicz.  We play classic acoustic hits that resonate with all ages. When our acoustic duo performs at your event, you can be sure everyone will recognize and love the music.
